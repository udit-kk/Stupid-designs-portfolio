Rishi Blends Brand Identity

Brand Identity Herbal Wellness 2024
Project Overview

Where Bold Meets Brew

Rishi Blends is a bold, youth-forward wellness brand built on a simple but powerful idea — you don't need beans for a great brew. The brand speaks to a new generation of coffee lovers who want flavour, energy, and attitude without the caffeine crash.

This project covers the complete brand identity — from the distinctive wordmark to a vibrant social media creative series that brings the brand to life.

"No beans. No drama. Just bold, barley-brewed goodness crafted for the ones who want it all — minus the jitters."

Logo Design

A Mark as Bold as the Blend

The Rishi Blends wordmark is a custom-crafted logotype with distinctive letterforms that feel modern, confident, and unforgettable. The typeface blends retro-Indian influences with bold, groovy curves — creating a mark that's immediately recognisable at any scale.

Social Media Creatives

A Feed That Stops the Scroll

Three high-energy social media creatives were designed to launch Rishi Blends' online presence — each with its own punchy headline and personality, while sharing the same vibrant, youthful visual DNA. From "Brew Your Vibe" to "No Beans. No Drama." — every post drives brand recall and engagement.

Packaging Design

Designed for the Premium Shelf

Four distinct superlatte flavours — each with its own colour-coded packaging identity. Bold label design, atmospheric gradient textures, and the iconic Rishi Blends wordmark on every pouch.
