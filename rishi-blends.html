Rishi Blends Brand Identity

Brand Identity Barley Coffee 2024 Udit Kumar Chauhan
Project Overview

Where Bold Meets Brew

Rishi Blends is a bold, youth-forward barley coffee brand built on a simple but powerful idea — you don't need beans to have a great brew. The brand speaks to a new generation of wellness-conscious consumers who want flavour, energy, and attitude without the caffeine crash.

This project covers the complete brand identity — from the distinctive wordmark and logo system to a vibrant social media creative series that brings the brand to life on Instagram and beyond.

"No beans. No drama. Just bold, barley-brewed goodness crafted for the ones who want it all — minus the jitters."

Logo Design

A Mark as Bold as the Blend

The Rishi Blends wordmark is a custom-crafted logotype with distinctive letterforms that feel modern, confident, and unforgettable. The typeface blends retro-Indian influences with bold, groovy curves — creating a mark that's immediately recognisable at any scale.
