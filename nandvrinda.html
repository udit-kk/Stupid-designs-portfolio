Brand Philosophy

Strength & Sacred Purity

Nandvrinda is a soulfully inspired essential oil brand born from ancient Indian wisdom, rooted in purity, tradition, and nature.

The name is a powerful fusion of two sacred symbols: 'Nand', representing the divine bull of Lord Shiva (Nandi) for strength and grounding, and 'Vrinda', the sacred Tulsi plant (Holy Basil) symbolizing purity and healing.

The brand philosophy honors tradition while embracing modern aesthetics, channeling spiritual energy into everyday wellness through therapeutic-grade oils.

Packaging Design

Premium Organic Labels

The label design translates the brand's sacred aesthetics into a high-end consumer product, utilizing natural textures, gold foil accents, and an earthy color system to distinguish each oil variant.
