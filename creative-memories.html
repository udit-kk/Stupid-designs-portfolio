Creative Memories

Photoshop Art Photo Manipulation 2024 Udit Kumar Chauhan
Project Overview

Where Photography Becomes Art

Creative Memories is a digital art piece that blurs the line between photography and painting — transforming real photographic moments into layered, dreamlike compositions through advanced Photoshop techniques.

Each element — light leaks, bokeh, painterly textures, and tonal adjustments — was deliberately chosen to evoke the feeling of nostalgia and creative wonder.

Tools & Process

The Art of Digital Compositing

Multiple exposure blending, painterly overlay techniques, and custom brush textures were layered together to create artwork that feels both photographic and hand-painted.
