Rishi Farms Branding

Branding Packaging 2024
Project Overview

An Organic Brand Rooted in Nature

Rishi Farms is an organic food brand built on the philosophy of clean, natural living. The challenge was to create a brand identity that felt authentic to the farm-to-table ethos while being premium enough to compete on modern retail shelves.

This project covers the complete brand ecosystem — from the logo mark and visual language to packaging for six distinct product lines and a full social media presence.

"Our company's mission is to process and manufacture products using clean, freshly harvested, and nutrition-rich ingredients without compromising the taste and the benefits of the products."
