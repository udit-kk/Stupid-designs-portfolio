Project Overview

For the Few Who Know

Chosen Kicks is a premium sneaker and streetwear brand built around exclusivity, culture, and bold self-expression. The brand needed an identity that speaks directly to sneakerheads and street culture enthusiasts — a mark that feels like a badge of honour.

The logo and visual identity system were designed to be instantly recognisable — clean, bold, and unapologetically confident.
